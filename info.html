About

Samir Hellal is a sound professional with experience in both film and music. He has created soundscapes for a variety of short films and documentaries, and has also mixed tracks for different artists. His work in sound design often centers on independent films and creative documentary projects. The productions he has been involved in have been presented at international festivals and reached audiences worldwide, earning recognition along the way.
